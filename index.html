Legendary Creature Collector

A tool to browse and track your collection of Magic: The Gathering legendary creatures, ordered by their first appearance in the game.

Search Syntax

  • t: Type line (e.g., t:dragon)
  • o: Oracle text (e.g., o:"draw a card")
  • c:wug or c=wug Exact colors
  • c>wg Colors include
  • c<wug Colors are a subset
  • s: Set code (e.g., s:dom)
  • d:2018 or d:2018-2020 Release year/range
  • r: Rarity (e.g., r:mythic)
  • is:owned Owned cards
  • ! Negate a filter (e.g., !t:goblin)
  • (term) and (term) AND operation
  • (term) or (term) OR operation
  • Group terms with parentheses for complex queries.
